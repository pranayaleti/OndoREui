    Property management built for Utah owners

    Leasing, screening, rent, maintenance, and reporting—with brokerage, loans, and notary when you need them in the same relationship.

    Whether you own one door or a growing portfolio, Ondo Real Estate runs day-to-day operations so you are not juggling spreadsheets, vendors, and tenant messages alone. We combine property management with the rest of our real estate stack—so sales, refinancing, and notarized documents do not require a separate cast of vendors.

    Marketing vacant units, showings, applications, and thorough tenant screening so you start with the right residents.

    Rent collection, owner statements, and clear financial reporting so you always know how the asset is performing.

    Coordinated repairs, vendor relationships, and documented work—plus support for Utah rental compliance expectations.

    Dedicated workflows for owners and tenants: fewer handoffs, faster responses, and a single partner accountable for outcomes.
