## Who We Are

Ondo Real Estate is a tech-forward, full-service real estate company based in Lehi, Utah. We bring brokerage, property management, mortgage lending, and notary services under one roof — backed by proprietary software and AI tools that give our clients an edge in the Utah market.

We serve the entire Wasatch Front: Salt Lake City, Provo, Lehi, Draper, Sandy, Orem, Ogden, St. George, and surrounding communities.
